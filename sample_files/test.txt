=== RiskGRC FinTech Security Assessment Report ===

Organization: ApexPay Microfinance Ltd.
Sector: FinTech
Assessment Period: Q2 2026

The organization recently completed a cybersecurity posture review focused on governance, risk, and compliance controls relevant to Pakistan’s fintech ecosystem.

Key findings from the review are summarized below:

- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) adoption across employee accounts is currently at 94%.
- Critical security patches are typically deployed within 11 days after release.
- All customer-sensitive databases and storage systems are encrypted using AES-256 standards.
- Failed authentication attempts represent approximately 3.1% of all login activity observed this month.
- The organization currently maintains 18 privileged/admin-level accounts.
- Average incident response and containment time is estimated at 5 hours.
- Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) mitigation services are active and operational through the upstream provider.
- Third-party vendor cyber risk was assessed as HIGH with a calculated risk score of 81/100.
- Annual cybersecurity awareness training completion has reached 89% among staff members.

Additional Notes:
- Several phishing attempts targeting finance employees were reported during April 2026.
- No major ransomware incidents were detected during the reporting period.
- Legacy VPN infrastructure is still used by a small subset of remote contractors.
- API gateways are protected through rate limiting and IP reputation filtering.
- SIEM alerts showed elevated brute-force attempts originating from foreign IP ranges.

Compliance Mapping:
- SBP Cybersecurity Framework: PARTIALLY COMPLIANT
- SECP FinTech Regulations: COMPLIANT
- PTA Data Protection Guidelines: COMPLIANT WITH OBSERVATIONS

Risk Summary:
The institution demonstrates a mature baseline cybersecurity posture but retains moderate operational risk due to vendor exposure and delayed patch deployment windows.
